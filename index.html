About

Who Are We?

Info Bot is a website with Java Programs in the Higher Education level.
The Website aims to make the life of a Computer Science student easier.
Most Programs can be found here, all created, compiled and verified by our team.

Why Choose Us?

Each Individual of the team has more than 2 years of Java to support them.
We do constant checks on all the Programs to make sure there are no errors.
Our Support Team makes sure to answer to questions in 1-2 days.
We make constant changes and updates based on User Reviews.
